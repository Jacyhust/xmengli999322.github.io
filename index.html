Biography [CV]

I am currently a forth year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree from the School of Mechano-Electronic Engineering, Xidian University. I worked with Prof. Kai Xie for undergraduate research.

My research interests include Deep Learning for Medical Image Analysis and 3D Vision. I am dedicated to bringing modern deep learning methods to biomedical image analysis. I also have expertise in deep learning for 3D shape analysis.
